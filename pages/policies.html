Policies

Vaccinations

Rabies

We require proof of rabies vaccination for your pup. If your pup cannot have a rabies shot for health reasons, we require a note from your vet that excuses them from the vaccination. Pups (ages 6 months or younger are exempt) from having to submit proof of rabies vaccination.

DHLP-Parvo (Distemper)

We required proof of DHLP-Parvo (Distemper) vaccination for your pup. Proof must include evidence that all rounds of the vaccine have been completed.

Bordetella (Kennel Cough)

We require proof of Bordetella vaccination for your pup.

Drop-Off

When dropping off/picking up your pup, we ask that you ring the video doorbell for assistance. Please keep your dog(s) on a leash or in a carrier when you come and go from the shop. We will call and text when your pup is ready to be picked up. We will do our absolute best to give you a 15-20 min heads up. We request that pet owners pick up their pups in a timely manner to ensure that other pups can get the same stress-free experience. Please notify us if you anticipate a delayed pick up and please contact us the day of if you are unable to pick up your pup in a timely manner.

Cancellations

Please try your best to let us know 24 hours in advance if you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment. We understand unexpected situations come up so don't hesitate to reach out if need be.

Please try your best to arrive on-time for your scheduled appointment. This will help make sure we have enough time to give your pup a the full spa day they deserve.

Services

Ear Cleaning

To protect your pups health, we do not offer any ear hair pulling services. Please seek care from your veterinarian if necessary.

Anal Glands

We perform external anal gland expressions to ensure your pup's safety. Typically this service is only needed for small dogs. If we encounter any issue during the expression we will let you know so that you can work with your veterinarian if necessary.
